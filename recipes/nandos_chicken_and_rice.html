Nandos's Style Peri-Peri Chicken and Rice

Home

Description

Nandos Peri Peri Chicken and Rice at home and even better! It is the most delicious one pan dinner that takes less than an hour to make and everyone will love. The dish is packed with flavour, spice, and color so what is not to love!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds chicken thighs boneless and skinless
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • medium peri peri sauce

Steps

  1. Marinade the chicken
  2. Cook the chicken
  3. Cook the rice
